2. **Reorganize Seating:** Place chairs around the dining table to create a functional dining area. Ensure there is enough space for comfortable movement around the table.
3. **Optimize Space Usage:** Utilize the corners and walls more effectively. For example, a sideboard can be placed along the east wall to store dining essentials.
4. **Enhance Visual Harmony:** Choose a consistent style and color scheme for the furniture to improve visual appeal. Arrange the furniture in a balanced manner to create a harmonious look.
5. **Consider Traffic Flow:** Ensure that the layout allows for easy movement within the room. Avoid placing furniture in a way that blocks pathways or creates congestion.
6. **Focus on Dining Functionality:** Prioritize the dining function by creating a clear focal point around the dining table. This will enhance the overall usability and purpose of the space.
